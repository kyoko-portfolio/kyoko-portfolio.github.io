Cinematic Trailer

Plushie Defense by Raccoopack Studios

Review

My most recent project that I’ve had a chance to work on was the cinematic trailer for Plushie Defense. 
The game was still going through some UI design changes so, before creating a gameplay trailer, I decided to create a cinematic trailer utilizing finalized assets. 
The goal was to make clear that the world is taking place inside the world of a crane machine, and show off the adorable cast of characters. I did some additional animations on top of the character animations by Art/Animation Lead, Pinia Chandara.
Plushie Defense takes a unique twist on tower defense where the enemy doesn’t just have one path! It will avoid your defense of plushies and dynamically path to your treasure. Collect Plushies and build the best team to defeat the claw!

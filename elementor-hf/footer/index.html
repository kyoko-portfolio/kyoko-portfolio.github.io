About Me

WHAT I DO

Telling a compelling story and maintaining a viewer’s attention can be difficult. My job as a freelance Video Editor is to efficiently convey your story to an audience in an entertaining and compelling way. Most importantly, while respecting your ideas and passion behind your content. I have experience editing trailers, interviews, devlogs, and dabble in (motion graphic) animation.

My Story

After working for several years in the Food and Beverage industry, I decided it wasn’t something I wanted to do long term. Creating videos was more a hobby, outside of electives, so it never crossed my mind to consider it a career until I volunteered at the BC Game Jam 2017.

 

I compiled event footage during my down time, and the organizers liked it so they showed it during the closing ceremonies! That led to some amazing oppertunities within the gaming industry, such as starting out as a video editor at Raccoopack Studios, and even had the oppertunity to work Brace Yourself Games, the accomplished team behind Crypt of the NecroDancer and Cadence of Hyrule. My most recent work includes some freelance work with Twitch Content Creators such as Ryan_Tuesday and DarkFiraTTV.

 

It has been quite the journey getting to this point and meeting some of the many talented and wonderful people in the industry. My current goal is to continuous learning and gaining experience within the media entertainment industry to make a place for myself in this amazing community.
